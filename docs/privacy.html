Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2025

Woof ("we," "our," or "the app") is a pet food scanning application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Camera Images: When you scan a pet food label, the image is sent to our servers for analysis. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently.

Scan History: We store your scan results (product name, scores, and analysis) so you can revisit them later. This data is associated with your account if you sign in.

Account Information: If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address. We use this solely for authentication and account management.

Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of scans, feature usage) to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your photo library
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze pet food ingredients and generate reports
  • To save your scan history for future reference
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of our analysis

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For ingredient analysis. Images are processed per their Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In: For authentication only

5. Data Retention

Scan images are processed in real-time and not stored beyond the analysis session. Scan results (text data) are cached for up to 7 days to provide faster results for repeated scans. Your scan history is retained as long as you have an active account.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen in the app. Account deletion is immediate and permanent. If you need assistance, contact us at woofapp.help@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Woof is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication (JWT), and server-side API key management. Your data is transmitted and stored securely.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:

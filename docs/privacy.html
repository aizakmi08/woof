Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Woof ("we," "our," or "the app") is a pet food scanning application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Camera Images: When you scan pet food packaging, ingredient labels, or human foods, the image is sent to our servers and AI providers for analysis. Images are processed in real time and are not intentionally stored after processing or saved in scan history.

Guest Usage Information: We use limited local quota and device/session information to provide guest scanning without requiring an account.

Search and Lookup Information: Product names, brands, barcode numbers, and search terms may be used to find matching product data.

Scan History: Scan results, including human-food safety details, are stored locally for guest users and may be synced to your account when you sign in.

Account Information: If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address. We use this solely for authentication and account management.

Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of scans, feature usage) to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your photo library
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze pet food ingredients and generate reports
  • To save your scan history for future reference
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To cache product analyses and reduce repeated AI processing
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of our analysis

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For ingredient analysis, safety checks, and scoring. Scan content may be processed per their Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: For product identification, OCR, and ingredient lookup assistance. Scan photos, product names, or label text may be processed per their Privacy Policy
  • Open Pet Food Facts: For product database lookups by barcode and product name.
  • ScrapingBee and public product websites: For server-side product lookup, when needed, to find public ingredient data.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In: For authentication only

5. Data Retention

Scan images are processed in real time and are not intentionally stored after processing. Guest scan history is stored on your device. Signed-in scan history and cached analyses are retained while your account is active. Scan history contains product or food names, scores, timestamps, human-food safety details, and related analysis metadata, not the original scan photos. Some de-identified cached product analyses may be retained to improve performance and avoid repeating AI analysis.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated account data at any time from the Profile screen in the app. Account deletion is permanent. If you need assistance, contact us at woofapp.help@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Woof is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use security measures including HTTPS encryption, native secure storage for authentication sessions where supported, server-side API key management, and row-level security policies on our database.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

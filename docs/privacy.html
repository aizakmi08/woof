Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Woof ("we," "our," or "the app") is a pet food and pet safety scanning application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Camera Images: When you scan a pet food label or human food item, the image is sent to our servers for analysis. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently by Woof.

Scan History: We store your scan results, such as product or food name, scores, safety level, analysis, scan mode, and scan date, so you can revisit them later. This data is associated with your guest profile or signed-in account.

Guest and Account Information: We create a guest account identifier when you use the app without signing in. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address. We use this solely for authentication and account management.

Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Woof Pro, subscription status and purchase events are processed by RevenueCat and the app stores so we can unlock paid features.

Apple Search Ads Attribution: On iOS, RevenueCat may collect Apple Search Ads attribution through Apple's AdServices framework so we can understand whether Apple Search Ads campaigns lead to app use or subscriptions. We do not use IDFA for this.

Operational Logs and Crash Reports: We may process limited technical information, such as request timing, errors, crash reports, app version, device/platform details, and service usage, to keep the app reliable, prevent abuse, and improve scan quality.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your photo library
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not use IDFA or retargeting advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze pet food ingredients, check human-food safety for pets, and generate reports
  • To save your scan history for future reference
  • To manage your guest profile, account, and subscription
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of our analysis

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For ingredient and food-safety analysis. Images are processed per their Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and Apple Search Ads attribution measurement. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Ads / AdServices: For Apple Search Ads attribution on iOS without IDFA
  • Sentry: For crash reporting and error diagnostics. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In: For optional authentication only
  • Open Pet Food Facts: For open pet food product data used to enrich some analyses

5. Data Retention

Scan images are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently by Woof. Shared analysis results may be cached for up to 7 days to provide faster results for repeated scans. Your scan history is retained as long as you keep your guest profile or signed-in account, unless you delete it in the app.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your guest data or account and associated data at any time from the Profile screen in the app. Deletion is immediate and permanent. It removes your account, scan history, scan usage records, linkable operational analytics, subscription webhook records associated with your app user ID, and local on-device scan/result caches. App Store or Google Play purchase records may remain with the store for billing and legal reasons. If you need assistance, contact us at woofapp.help@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Woof is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication (JWT), and server-side API key management. Your data is transmitted and stored securely.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:
